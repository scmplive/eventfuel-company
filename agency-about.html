About Giorgio Asia - Luxury Events & Productions

Giorgio Asia Group is the fastest growing experiential event and visual merchandising agency in the Hong Kong & Greater China region.

We work closely with ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certified suppliers in Dongguan, PRC, with all compatible manufacturing facilities, such as MDF & Acrylic cutting, CNC machines, laser cutting machines, silk screen & pack printing machines, providing all sources of materials like MDF, plywood, acrylic, laminate, leatherette, PU and etc.

Orchestrating the complex series of actions needed to make an event run flawlessly has become the expertise of the team, while the creative insights brought by the personnel make GIORGIO one of the most sought after event agencies in the region for luxury brands.
