Visual Merchandising

GIORGIO provides a global solution for our customers, starting from the detailed study of a concept, through to development and prototyping, to the production and product delivery. Our Hong Kong based client servicing team ensures quality control which spans the purchase and approval of materials to final installation, With their eye for detail and experience working with luxury brands, our Chinese-speaking staff visit the factories regularly, ensuring that the highest standards are adhered to.

Regardless of size, we always produce mock-ups and full-scale prototypes for final client approval before production. We are happy to accompany our clients to visit the factory and view the prototypes for themselves.
